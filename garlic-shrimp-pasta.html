Garlic Shrimp Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 8oz spaghetti
  • 3/4lb medium shrimp
  • 1tsp Old Bay seasoning
  • 1/2tsp crushed red pepper
  • 2tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic - finely chopped
  • 2/3cup white wine
  • 2tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4cup butter
  • 1/2cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2cup chicken broth
  • 1/4cup parsley
  • salt/pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water
  2. Season shrimp with Old Bay seasoning, red pepper flakes, and salt and pepper
  3. Heat oil in large skillet. Add shrimp and garlic and cook for 2 minutes, turn over and cook 1 minute. Remove shrimp and set aside
  4. Add wine and lemon juice to deglaze pan. Add butter, cream, and chicken broth. Simmer 5 minutes or until slightly reduced
  5. Drain spaghetti, reserve 1 cup of pasta water
  6. Return the shrimp to the skillet, stir in pasta and parsley, and cook until heated through, add pasta water if needed
