Shackshuka Recipe

Ingredients:

  • olive oil and/or butter
  • onion
  • peppers
  • garlic
  • tomatoes
  • eggs
  • fresh herbs (parsley, cilantro, basil work well)
  • crushed nuts

Instructions:

  1. bring oil to medium/high heat in a sautee pan
  2. sautee roughly chopped onions and peppers until onions are translucent
  3. add diced garlic to pan and sautee just long enough to release fragrances - 1 minute max
  4. add roughly diced tomatoes to pan and continue sauteeing until the liquids have cooked down - ~10 minutes
  5. while the veggies sautee uncovered, chop the herbs and nuts and mix them with salt and pepper
  6. when the veggies are ready, draw circles with your spatula the size of an egg and crack the egg into the circle
  7. immediately cover the pan with a lid and cook eggs sunny side up style/easy
  8. serve on a plate with herb/nut mix as a topper with bread or pita or whatever you like
